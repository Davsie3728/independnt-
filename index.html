Registered Nursing

Why?

I would love to become a registered nurse because there are so many jobs I could choose from. I could work with anyone, from newborns to the elderly. Because a registered nurse must be knowledgeable of the entire body and it's system. There are many fields I could choose from depending on what sparks my interest the most. Anywhere from a physician's office to the hospital. Helping others is a feeling of accomplishment that I can not describe, and to be able to do that on a regular basis would mean a lot whether it's helping care for a child at birth or making the last few days of someone's life comfortable. Not only would I help someone, I would be creating a relationship with my community.

What do I need?

A degree of at the minimum Associates, or you could get your Bachelors degree. After you graduate and pass all of your needed classes you have to then pass a state nursing exam. Once you do those things you are then certified as a registered nurse.

Jobs I can get?

Well after you pass your certification exam and get your degree, you are qualified for any nursing job. As I said in my 'Why?' section, there is a wide range of options. You can work with newborns that have just been brought into this world to making someones last few days on the earth comfortable when they say goodbye. From a Hospital to a Office. You like a fast pace? Go look into the ER. You like a slower pace? There's many doctor offices around. Want to work with kids or elderly? Go to a children hospital or a pediatric office, or go to a retirement home.
